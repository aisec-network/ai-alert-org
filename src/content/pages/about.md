AI Alert is an incident and vulnerability tracker for AI and ML systems. Model leaks, training-data exposures, jailbreak disclosures, ML CVEs, and vendor security advisories — logged with primary sources and dates.

## What we track

- **AI/ML incidents** — model leaks, training-data breaches, and adversarial misuse with real-world impact
- **Vulnerability disclosures** — CVEs in ML frameworks and AI infrastructure, with severity and patch status
- **Jailbreak disclosures** — new techniques disclosed by researchers, with model coverage and timeline
- **Vendor advisories** — security updates from AI labs and ML platform vendors
- **Incident analysis** — structured post-mortems where primary-source documentation exists

## Who reads this

Security teams, ML engineers, and researchers who need a reliable, sourced record of what is actually breaking in deployed AI systems. Every entry is linked to a primary source — no unattributed claims.

## Stay current

Subscribe to the RSS feed for real-time incident alerts. To report a new AI/ML incident or vulnerability, email the editorial desk with a primary-source link and we will review for inclusion.
